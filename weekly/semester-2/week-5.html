disst.

Last week, Joselyn reminded us that we should somewhat finalise our discussion points by the first consultation, so we can get constructive feedback for it and continue working on it through the following week. Gideon has also given us some feedback on how our studio can improve. For my projects, he mentioned that my take on “uncovering the attributes of design thinking” could be presented stronger and clearer by expanding on why am I doing it, for example “as a way of questioning its relevance today”. I could take this comment onto my dissertation discussion points.

Previously, my discussion was split into 3 points:

1. How are design thinking processes impacting the design industry in terms of communication between teams and stakeholders?

2. How can young designers adapt better to the design industry with the help of design thinking guides that are less rigid? What are some benefits young designers can get from systematic design thinking guides?

3. I would like to nudge my take on the subject more towards the systematic side.
