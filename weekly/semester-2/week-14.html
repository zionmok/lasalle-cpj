A wise man once said, copying is how we learn. “You start when you're young and you copy. You straight up copy.”

For the first few frames, I adapted the animation style from Apple's Don't Blink ad, and figure out how to do it on AE.

The rest is history...

Started out okay with the few animations I adopted from watching kinetic text advertisements on Youtube, but soon, I find myself looking at static texts, not knowing what to do with them.

At first, I overcame the creative block by simply reusing the same animation again. And slowly, more inspiration came to me, like the slanted “angle” sliding in.

Working Smarter
