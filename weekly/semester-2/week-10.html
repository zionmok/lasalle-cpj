Mid-Fi Prototype

Inspired by the homepage of the app store, I wanted to design something where users can explore new categories and popular projects.

app store


wildebeest

One of the special features of the app would be the search function. Through the user interview I’ve conducted, there are many instances where young designers are unable to find examples of a certain type of design when they already have a rough image in their minds. Therefore, I would like to introduce a system where it speaks the language of young designers in SG. Using keywords that are familiar to the users, such as the target audience, problems, and pain points, they will have an easier time finding what they are looking for, thus ideating is made efficient.

Wildebeest

Upon searching with keywords, users can access the thought process of each project in the app.

    Seminars
    Previous Next

    This week was filled with seminars from female designers. Some of these were organised by Lasalle, and others were received in my inbox. Nonetheless, all these seminars share one common thread, which is how female designers can make an impact in the design industry as the gender with greater empathy nerves (scientifically).

    One area that I’ve always struggled with is the ability to construct sentences, which include questions. But after attending more seminars in my time in Lasalle, I find myself observing and learning from the way these hosts ask questions, and developed my critical thinking skills which in turn allow me to construct my questions in a way that makes sense and helps to get the answer I’m looking for.
