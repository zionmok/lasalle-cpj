P-Board


A combination of past works and examples from platforms such as Pinterest, I compiled a few graphic design p-boards for inspiration.

Mainly the ones with mock-ups attracted my attention. I am convinced that a p-board will not come to life until the design is superimposed on a mockup, which is also why it’s so easy to impress with the design outcome instead of the process.

A particular graphic for a mobile app caught my attention. It was from runningstories.app.

I especially enjoy the layer of elements that pop out from the mobile, showing the depth of the app.

What is fascinating about it is that it wasn’t from a p-board per se. This goes to show that inspiration can come from anywhere.
